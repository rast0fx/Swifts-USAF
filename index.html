Our Commands

The United States Air Force is organized into several major commands, each with distinct missions and responsibilities.

"Defensor Fortis - Defender of the Force"

Security Forces

The U.S. Air Force Security Forces (SF) are the military police and ground defense force of the Air Force. They protect bases, enforce laws, and respond to threats and emergencies.

"Voice of the Air Force"

Public Affairs

Air Force Public Affairs manages communication, media relations, and international diplomacy for the U.S. Air Force. They build alliances, handle public messaging, and maintain the Air Force‚Äôs image worldwide.

"We Go Where You Go"

Air Force Exchange

The Air Force Exchange ensures airmen's uniforms are properly fitted, comfortable, and professional in appearance. They help maintain a sharp, consistent image across the Air Force.

Active

"Any Time, Any Place"

Special Operations Command

Air Force Special Operations Command (AFSOC) provides highly trained, rapidly deployable forces for global special operations missions. Their roles include precision strikes, infiltration, support, and intelligence operations to support unified combatant commands.

Active

"Global Power for America"

Air Combat Command

The Air Combat Command (ACC) is a major United States Air Force command responsible for organizing, training, and equipping air combat forces. Its primary mission is to provide combat-ready forces for air, space, and cyberspace operations. ACC oversees various aircraft, including fighter jets, bombers, reconnaissance aircraft, and remotely piloted vehicles. These forces ensure air superiority, global strike capabilities, and intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (ISR) support.

Active

"Serving Those Who Serve"

Community Staff

The Community Staff (CS) operates under the Air Staff+ and is responsible for maintaining the group‚Äôs image and safeguarding the community. It oversees the Moderation Team, which helps enforce rules and ensure a safe server environment. The Events Team, also part of CS, organizes activities to keep the community engaged and active.

Active

"Aim High... Fly-Fight-Win"

Air Education and Training Command

Air Education & Training Command (AETC) is responsible for transforming civilians into Airmen and developing future leaders of the U.S. Air Force. They provide essential training and education to prepare personnel for mission readiness and leadership roles.
